Frequently Asked Questions

How does FollowPulse work?
FollowPulse analyzes publicly available data from your Instagram account to track follower changes. We compare your current followers with previous snapshots to identify who unfollowed you.
Do you need my Instagram password?
No, we never ask for or store your Instagram password. We only access publicly available profile information.
Why can I only track my own account?
To prevent misuse and protect user privacy, we only allow tracking of accounts you own. This is verified through our account confirmation process.
How often is data updated?
We update your follower data daily. Pro users can request manual refreshes more frequently.
What's the difference between Free and Pro?
Free plan supports accounts with up to 1,000 followers. Pro plan ($4.99/month) supports up to 30,000 followers and includes advanced analytics, data export, and priority support.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings in the app and tap "Delete Account". This will permanently remove all your data from our servers.
Why is there a 24-hour wait to change accounts?
This limit helps prevent abuse of our service and ensures fair usage for all users.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone to manage or cancel your subscription.
